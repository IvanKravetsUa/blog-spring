About us

My Dream blog is an information and entertainment community, all content of which is created by users. The site administration only keeps order and compliance with the rules. All added news and comments have their rating. News on the main page is formed by a special algorithm that displays the most relevant posts at the moment in the top. Your rating increases with each plus received for your news and comments. Each vote for the post changes your rating by 2 points, and for a comment - by 1 point. With the growth of your rating, you open up new opportunities. Dependence of your capabilities on your rating:

-300 Your account is automatically blocked;
-200 You can not add comments;
0 Your rating at registration;
+10 You can attach a picture to the comment. Links in your comments become clickable;
+150 You can add video;
+10000 can combine tags.

Depending on the rating for the day, you can add:

200 and below 1 post
200-500 2 posts
500-1000 3 posts
1000-3000 4 posts
3000 и более 5 posts
